A literature review was carried out to examine existing cryptographic, deniable, and cloud-based filesystems, as well as state-of-the-art security standards. This created a basis for the technologies and security principles used in the produced filesystem, including \gls{FUSE} as the filesystem library. Furthermore, experiments were carried out to gather quantitative data used to compare the performance of the produced filesystem against other relevant filesystems.
